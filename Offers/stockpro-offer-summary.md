## Offer Stack

### Core (included in both tiers)

**Position-Aware AI Research Reports**
AI research calibrated to trade type (Day / Swing / Investment) and contextualized to the user's cost basis and position size. Not a generic ticker report — a report that tells you what this stock means for your specific position. No competitor can claim this.

**Portfolio Tracker — Stocks + Crypto**
The data layer that makes personalization possible. P&L, cost basis, holdings across stocks and crypto in one place. This is what enables every position-aware feature in the stack.

**Watchlist + Contextual Price Alerts**
Alerts that reference the research, not just price thresholds. When a stock moves, the context is already there.

**Filtered News + CSV Brokerage Import**
News filtered to holdings and watchlist tickers only — no noise from the broader market. Import from any brokerage in 60 seconds via CSV.

## Key Insights From the Workshop

**The real moat is not the AI.** It's the accumulated data. Portfolio history, research history, investment theses. Every product decision should increase switching cost. The AI pipeline commoditizes in 12–18 months. The user's own research history never does.

**Pain reframe was the critical Phase 1 fix.** "Consolidate your tools" is a vitamin — it describes a workflow improvement. "Stop making trades you haven't properly researched" is a painkiller — it describes a loss. All marketing copy must lead with the performance outcome, not the workflow efficiency.

**Proof is the fatal gap, not the product.** The product works. The research pipeline is real. The Perceived Likelihood score sits at 7/10 because there are no users yet and no testimonials. The bridge is a static pre-generated sample report and backtested case studies. This must be built before any meaningful acquisition effort.

**Free beta is the right opening move.** It removes purchase risk for early users, generates real usage data, and seeds word-of-mouth with people who've actually experienced the product. The risk is a weak day-14 conversion trigger. Build the email sequence before opening beta.

**Marcus validated the offer.** He's the hardest persona to convert — evidence-driven, Seeking Alpha dropout, will trial before paying with no exceptions. He scored 8/10 to convert after beta. The Thesis Vault was the feature that surprised him. The Pre-Earnings Alert he called "not a bonus — a core feature you're giving away." That's the signal: it's priced correctly.
