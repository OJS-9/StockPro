1Business Overview

NVIDIA Corporation designs and manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) and system-on-chip units. Originally serving gaming markets, NVIDIA has successfully pivoted to become the dominant infrastructure provider for artificial intelligence workloads, commanding an estimated 80-90% share of the AI training accelerator market.

The company operates across three segments: Data Center (72% of revenue), Gaming (19%), and Professional Visualization (5%). The Data Center segment is the primary growth engine and the focus of this analysis.
