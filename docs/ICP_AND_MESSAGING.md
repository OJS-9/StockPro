# StockPro — ICP & Messaging

> Defined: 2026-05-13
> Status: Pre-launch GTM ramp. 0 users. Three-ICP ABM strategy powered by in-house GTM platform + personal brand on LinkedIn/X.

---

## 1. Primary ICP — The Self-Directed Thoughtful Retail Investor

### Demographics
- Age 28-45
- College-educated
- Income $80k-$250k
- Portfolio $25k-$500k, self-managed brokerage
- Geo: US, UK, Canada, Australia, Israel (English-speaking)

### Psychographics
- Treats investing as a serious hobby, not gambling
- Reads 10-Ks, earnings calls, and analyst reports — but feels behind
- Suspicious of finfluencers, but still consumes them to stay current
- Wants an edge but knows they can't beat institutions on speed

### Jobs To Be Done
1. "Help me decide if this ticker deserves a place in my portfolio."
2. "Tell me what I'm missing that the crowd already knows."
3. "Track my conviction over time so I don't fool myself."

### Pains
- Research takes 3-6 hours per ticker, scattered across 8 browser tabs
- Sentiment is invisible until it's already priced in
- No memory between research sessions — re-doing work constantly

---

## 2. Secondary ICP — University Finance Club Members

Same psychographics as the primary, just younger / broker / more career-motivated. Treated as **real paying customers today** (lowest paid tier), not just a future funnel.

### Differences from primary
- Lower disposable income → lowest tier
- Higher career motivation → resume signal + skill-building matters
- Concentrated in clubs → multi-user-per-touch acquisition
- Future LTV is significant (graduate into the primary ICP)

### What this ICP values that primary doesn't
- Research outputs that "look institutional" (resume bait)
- Cohort/peer features (see what your club is researching)
- Paper-trading competition readiness

## 3. Messaging Pillars

Five pillars total. **Lead with #1 and #2** — those are the true differentiators. The rest reinforce.

| # | Pillar | One-liner |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Crowd intelligence** | "See what the crowd sees, before it moves the price." Reddit + X sentiment distilled per ticker. |
| 2 | **Institutional-grade research** | "Institutional-grade research without the institutional team." AI does the analyst work. |
| 3 | **Second brain** | "Your second brain for investing." Research saved, searchable, builds conviction over time. |
| 4 | **Consolidation** | "Multi-agent, not multi-tab." One platform replaces 8 browser tabs. |
| 5 | **Anti-WSB positioning** | "Built for the thoughtful retail investor, not the day-trading degenerate." |

### Positioning rules
- Always pair pillar #1 or #2 with the secondary message.
- Never use casino / lottery / "moonshot" framing — violates pillar #5.
- For the secondary ICP (uni clubs), reweight toward pillar #2 (institutional-grade) for resume-signal value.

## 4. Where the ICP Lives — Channel Priority

### Top 3 to start (no Reddit, low friction, ICP-dense)

| Rank | Channel | Why | Sub-segments |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **FinTwit on X** | Dense ICP, real-time, brand-hat already operates there | $cashtag posters, stock-pickers, earnings-season threaders |
| 2 | **LinkedIn** | Finance-adjacent professionals, MBA students, ex-IB now in tech — retail content travels well | Finance professionals who invest personally |
| 3 | **University finance clubs** | Direct/relational, repeatable, multi-user per touch | Club officers, paper-trading competitors, CFA candidates |

### Roadmap channels (later)
- **Reddit** (r/investing, r/SecurityAnalysis, r/ValueInvesting) — deprioritized: accounts need karma/maturity before posting works
- **Substack comment sections** — readers of The Diff, Stratechery, Net Interest, Doomberg
- **YouTube finance creator communities** — Joseph Carlson, Plain Bagel, Damodaran, Patrick Boyle
- **Discord servers** — paid creator-run + free investing communities
- **Stocktwits** — useful but skews day-trader
- **Bogleheads.org** — high-intent but skews passive, may resist active tooling
- **Country-specific forums** — UK MoneySavingExpert investing board, Aussie HotCopper, IL community forums
- **CFA candidate communities** — exact-fit for secondary ICP

### Channels explicitly out
- **WSB and any meme-stock community** — violates pillar #5
- **Generic personal-finance forums** (budgeting, debt) — wrong intent
